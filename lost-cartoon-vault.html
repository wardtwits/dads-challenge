Secret Agent Mission

The Lost Cartoon Vault

The Cartoon Vault has locked itself. Inside is the name of a missing show. To open it, solve each puzzle and write the Agent Letter from every puzzle into the final vault strip.

Do the puzzles in order. Each one gives you a letter for the final reveal and points you to the next puzzle.

Materials Needed

  • Pencil and eraser
  • Scratch paper
  • Colored pencil or marker for crossing things out
  • Optional: scissors to cut out the final vault strip

Mission time: about 60-120 minutes.

Parent Hint Cards

  1. Add all four answers first. Then subtract 24.
  2. Try placing Echo in each middle spot, then check Nova.
  3. Look at how much the numbers increase each time.
  4. Use the alphabet number chart.
  5. Move each coded letter 3 letters backward in the alphabet.
  6. Find row 1 first, then column 1.
  7. The word means information or numbers.
  8. The pattern repeats every three shapes.
  9. Use the first letter of each line.
  10. Four words are directions.
  11. Cross out A, V, L, and T because they are in VAULT.

Complete Answer Key for Parents

  1. 36 / 6 = 6, 9 + 4 = 13, 21 - 14 = 7, 8 x 2 = 16. Total 42. 42 - 24 = 18. A1Z26 gives R.
  2. The only order is Echo, Nova, Atlas, Blink. Spot 1 is Echo, so the letter is E.
  3. The increases are +2, +3, +4, +5, +6, so the next number is 29. 29 - 22 = 7. A1Z26 gives G.
  4. 21 = U.
  5. OHWWHU shifted back 3 becomes LETTER. The first letter is L.
  6. Coordinate 1,1 is A.
  7. DARRA unscrambles to RADAR. The first letter is R.
  8. The missing shape is Square, so the letter is S.
  9. The first letters spell HOW. Use the first letter of the hidden word for H.
  10. Orange is not a direction, so the letter is O.
  11. Crossing out letters from VAULT leaves W.
